Why all models struggle with Grade 8 Math

This dataset includes many repeating decimal-to-fraction conversions (e.g., "0.551(35)" → "54584/99000") and complex fraction operations. These problems require precise algorithmic computation rather than reasoning—a known weakness of language models. The "Functions" subcategory was particularly challenging, with all models scoring below 4%.
