Key Takeaways

Qwen3:4b leads overall with 86.4% accuracy and leads Prob & Stats (97.5%), while still being slower than Gemma and Phi at ~21.4 seconds per question.

Gemma3:4b excels at probability with 94.7% accuracy on Prob & Stats—the highest single-dataset score—and scores perfectly on limit evaluation problems.

Phi3:3.8b has the lowest accuracy on every dataset in the latest runs (2.5%–12.2%), despite relatively low response latency.

Grade 8 Math flipped from weakest to strong for Gemma and Qwen (67.5% and 78.3% respectively), while Phi remains a major outlier at 2.5%.
